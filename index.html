About

Frenyz Salon

“Rooted in the unwavering passion and dedication of a mother-daughter duo, Freny Beauty Parlour narrates a tale of beauty that took root in 1989. Jigisha harbored aspirations for Freny to blossom into a fashion designer from a young age. Freny Chauhan embarked on her journey by supporting her mother at Freny Beauty Parlour. As Freny matured, dreams expanded, and the vision evolved. Jigisha’s fervent desire to venture into fashion design ignited Freny Chauhan’s transformation into a visionary designer. Together, they birthed Frenyz Couture in 2017.

Frenyz Salon

Today, Frenyz Couture stands tall as an iconic presence in Gujarat, catering to a global clientele that extends far beyond borders. Our story transcends mere fashion; it encapsulates cherished family values, relentless dedication, and an unwavering commitment to offering a comprehensive solution in the fashion and grooming landscape.

Each family member contributes their unique expertise, forging a perfect fusion of fashion and grooming. Dad, Jay, spearheads our Human Resources and Accounts department, while son Yash Chauhan, and Dheeraj Yaduvanshi bring not only salon expertise but also proficiency in salon management and artistic finesse to the forefront.

The creative synergy between Mom, Freny Chauhan, Yash, and Dheeraj propels our fashion aspirations forward, setting trends and crafting styles that resonate with a global audience. Our collective goal now extends to offering superior styling and grooming advice in the industry.

Welcome to Frenyz Couture and Salon, where our family’s passion converges with your fashion and grooming desires, curating an unparalleled destination for all facets of style and beauty.
